Tips for a good scan

  • Fill the frame with the ingredients list.
  • Bright, even light — avoid glare on shiny packaging.
  • Hold steady, tap to focus until the text is sharp.
  • Add a second photo of the nutrition table for the best grade.

About FooLab

FooLab reads food and drink labels with AI and gives you a NutriScore grade plus red-flag alerts — palm oil, artificial colors, controversial additives, high sugar or salt.

Privacy: scans live only on this phone. No account, no server database. Only the photo you take is sent to the AI for analysis and then discarded.

Not medical advice. Use alongside your own judgement and any dietary guidance from your doctor.
