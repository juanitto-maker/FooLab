How to scan well

  2. The ingredients list — close-up, sharp text, fill the frame.
  3. The nutrition table — for an accurate NutriScore.

About FooLab

FooLab reads food and drink labels with AI and gives you a NutriScore grade plus red-flag alerts — palm oil, artificial colors, controversial additives, high sugar or salt.

Privacy: your scan archive lives only on this phone. The photo you capture is sent to the AI for analysis and discarded after.

Public catalog: when you save a scan, you can tick "Let the world know about your finding" to add the card and a square thumbnail to the shared catalog so other shoppers can find the product. The toggle defaults on but you can switch it off for any scan, or every scan, and nothing leaves your phone beyond the AI analysis.

Not medical advice. Use alongside your own judgement and any dietary guidance from your doctor.
